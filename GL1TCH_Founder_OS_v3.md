# 3. Product & Token Strategy

## 3.1 What GL1TCH Is
A Solana SPL token + a premium brand + exactly one flagship utility (holder-gated content & ranks). Not a security, not an investment contract — a community/brand/meme project (see Section 12).

## 3.6 Tokenomics (ZERO-TAX)

**Core parameters**
- Ticker: **$GL1TCH**
- Chain: **Solana** (SPL Token)
- Total Supply: **1,000,000,000** (1B) — fixed, fully minted at launch, **no inflation ever**.
- Decimals: **6** (Pump.fun standard for Path A).
- **Buy/sell tax: 0%.** No transaction tax, ever.
- **Mint authority: revoked.** No new tokens can ever be created.
- **Freeze authority: revoked.** No wallet can ever be frozen.
- **Liquidity: burned or locked** (see 3.11). FDV ≈ market cap intent — **no hidden unlocks, no presale, no vesting cliffs to dump.**

**Distribution — Path A (Pump.fun fair launch — DEFAULT)**
On Pump.fun the bonding curve distributes supply to the open market. There is **no pre-allocation and no presale**.
- ~100% of supply distributed fairly via the bonding curve to the public.
- The team funds treasury/marketing/give-back via a **small, fully-disclosed dev buy on the open curve at the same price as everyone else** — capped at **≤ 3%** and announced publicly before/at launch.
- No hidden team wallets. The dev-buy wallet address is published on `/links`.

**Distribution — Path B (self-managed Raydium/Meteora LP — FUTURE, optional)**
If/when the project migrates to a self-managed pool, this conceptual allocation applies:

| Allocation | % | Purpose | Handling |
|---|---|---|---|
| Public Liquidity | 50% | DEX pool | LP burned/locked |
| Community | 20% | Rewards, quests, give-back | Transparent wallet |
| Marketing | 15% | Growth, listings, KOLs | Transparent wallet |
| Treasury | 10% | Runway, ops | Multisig |
| Team | 5% | Contributors | Locked/vested, disclosed |

> Path A is the launch reality. Path B numbers are documented for transparency and only activate on migration.

**Treasury funding without a tax:** funded by the disclosed dev buy (Path A) and/or the Treasury allocation (Path B). Never by a transaction tax.

## 3.11 Launch & Trust Strategy

- **Launch path:** **Pump.fun first** (fair, fast, low-friction), with **optional future migration** to a self-managed Raydium/Meteora LP once volume/community justify it.
- **Fair-launch principles:** no presale, no private allocation, no hidden team wallets. Any team buy is small, disclosed, and on the open curve.
- **Anti-snipe posture:** the contract address (CA) is revealed **simultaneously** across X, Telegram, and the site `/links` page to remove insider sniping advantage.
- **Trust hardening (all proven on-chain):**
  - Mint authority revoked, freeze authority revoked.
  - LP burned or time-locked (Pump.fun auto-migrates LP to Raydium at the bonding-curve threshold; for Path B, burn/lock explicitly).
  - **External verification: RugCheck.xyz + SolSniffer** scores published at launch (our Solana equivalent of FLOKI's CertiK). A paid independent audit is **not** required for the base token; reconsider only if a future utility contract holds funds (it does not for the launch utility — see 3.12).
  - Live **Trust Wall** on the site (authorities, LP, holders, external score, FDV note) reading real state, never hardcoded.

## 3.12 Flagship Utility (SINGLE — DECIDED): Holder-Gated Content + Ranks

We ship **exactly one** utility. Staking-lite is explicitly **out of scope** for launch.

**What it is:** Wallet-linked rank tiers (mapped to 2.4) that unlock private lore, content, and Telegram rooms based on verified on-chain holdings. **No custody of user funds** — the system only reads balances and grants access/roles.

**Verification flow:**
1. User connects a Solana wallet (Phantom/Solflare/Backpack) on the site or via the Telegram bot.
2. System reads the wallet's $GL1TCH balance on-chain.
3. Rank is granted based on thresholds; Telegram roles / gated content unlock accordingly.

**Holding thresholds (initial — defined as % of the 1B supply so they hold regardless of price; fine-tune after launch once the holder distribution is visible):**

| Rank | Threshold (% of supply) | Threshold (tokens) | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|

| Infected | 0.01% | 100,000 | Holder badge, holder-only channel |
| Signal Bearer | 0.1% | 1,000,000 | Creator channel, raid coordination, early lore drops |
| Core Node | 0.5% | 5,000,000 | Inner strategy room, governance signals |
| Ghost Node | 1% | 10,000,000 | Top-tier room, direct line to core, exclusive archive |

**Delivery milestone:** ranks framework live in Phase 2 (off-chain roles), full wallet-gated verification live at/just after launch (Phase 3).

**Security note:** because no contract custodies funds, the utility carries minimal smart-contract risk — a deliberate advantage over staking models.
