## The differentiators to BUILD

### Tier 1 — unique, high-impact, buildable now
- **A. The Rogue-AI verdict (LLM narrative).** A short, in-character paragraph: the AI
  reads the chain and tells you in its *own words* what it thinks and what would have
  to go wrong. No competitor has a real AI-analyst voice — and it IS our brand. On-demand
  ("Ask GL1TCH") + cached to control cost.
- **B. Degen intelligence (bundle / sniper / insider / dev).** The stuff that actually
  rugs people: % supply that's **bundled** (wallets funded by one source), **sniped**
  (bought block 0-1), **insider**, and **dev/creator holdings**. We already pull RugCheck
  `topHolders.insider` + creator — surface it cleanly with plain-English "why this matters".
  This is the #1 thing Solana degens check today; we'd present it best.
- **C. Deployer track record.** "This wallet created 6 tokens — 4 went to zero." Check the
  *human*, not just the contract. Almost no consumer tool surfaces this front-and-center.

### Tier 2 — trust + distribution
- **D. Multi-source transparency.** "4 sources checked — RugCheck/GoPlus/DexScreener/chain.
  Here's where they disagree." Trust through receipts; nobody shows their work.
- **E. Side-by-side compare.** Scan two tokens, compare verdicts. Novel UX for "which do I ape".
- **F. Public API + embeddable widget.** Other projects embed "Scanned by GL1TCH" → backlinks,
  users, brand everywhere. Distribution flywheel.
- **G. Shareable verdict card / per-scan OG image.** Every scan becomes a tweet. Virality.

### Tier 3 — moat + $GL1TCH utility
- **H. Watchtower++** — wallet/portfolio watch, whale-exit alerts, dev-sells-supply alerts.
- **I. Holder-gated PRO tier** — unlimited watches, API key, priority alerts. Real demand for $GL1TCH.

## The positioning line
