# GL1TCH — Ethical 2-Week Go-To-Market Plan

> Honest starting point (live data 2026-07-12): $2,443 mcap · $1,938 liquidity · **2 holders** ·
> $0 24h volume · LP 100% locked · LOW RISK. Product is world-class; the **market and the audience
> don't exist yet.** No 2-week plan turns this into a moonshot — and anyone promising that is lying.
> The realistic 2-week goal: **go from "dead" to "alive"** — first real holders, first outside usage,
> a consistent presence, and teed-up revenue conversations. No wash trading, no fake volume, no paid
> pumps — those kill projects and violate our rules.

## The one hard truth to internalize first

You cannot get "returns" on an asset with $1.9K liquidity and 2 holders — there is no market to
return anything. So Week-1 priority #1 is **make it a tradeable, discoverable market**; everything
else ladders off that. The product/trust/features are DONE — the missing pieces are **liquidity,
people, and usage.**

## Roles

- **AUTONOMOUS (Claude runs, free):** organic reach — X reply engine on a cadence, daily content/
  video rotation, SEO cluster, proof-of-usage posts using REAL numbers (e.g. "1,953 tokens scanned").
- **FOUNDER (only you can):** liquidity, community nucleus, BD outreach (using the Outreach Kit),
  and any decision that moves funds or makes a public commitment.

## Week 1 — Wake it up

**Day 1–2 · Foundations (FOUNDER)**
- [ ] Decide the liquidity plan. With $1.9K, even small buys slip badly. Either add liquidity to a
      real depth you can afford (locked), or accept it stays micro and focus on utility+community.
      Be honest with yourself about budget. (No fake volume to simulate depth.)
- [ ] Stand up the community nucleus: pin a clear "what GL1TCH is" post on X + Telegram (free rug
      scanner + provably-fair randomness + agent trust — with the live links). Make the TG joinable.
- [ ] Set a daily presence commitment (30–60 min): reply in Solana/security/agent communities as a
      helpful person, not a shiller.

**Day 1–7 · Organic engine (AUTONOMOUS)**
- [ ] Run the X reply engine 1–2×/day (MAX=3, value-first) — helpful replies to real rug/scam
      questions, funneling to the free scanner. (Already verified working.)
- [ ] Post 1 piece/day rotating the rendered videos (randomness, product-tour, scanner, signal-graph,
      agent-trust) to X/YT/IG — consistency > volume.
- [ ] Publish 1 more SEO article to the randomness/safety clusters mid-week.
- [ ] One "proof of real usage" post using true numbers (scans, deployers mapped) — credibility.

**Day 3–7 · BD seeding (FOUNDER, using the Outreach Kit)**
- [ ] Send 5–10 personalized, value-first messages/day from the Outreach Kit — start with giveaway/
      raffle runners and agent builders (lowest-friction "just try the free tool" asks).
- [ ] Track every send + reply in the pipeline sheet.

**End of Week 1 — honest checkpoint:** Are there ANY holders beyond you? Any outside scans/draws?
Any reply to a BD message? If zero across all three, the bottleneck is reach/liquidity, not product —
adjust (more communities, reconsider liquidity, or a small ethical promo budget).

## Week 2 — Convert & compound

**Content + reach (AUTONOMOUS)**
- [ ] Keep the daily cadence. Double down on whatever got the most engagement in Week 1.
- [ ] Turn any real usage into a case study post ("a project ran a provably-fair giveaway with our
      proof link" — with the shareable proof).

**Community (FOUNDER)**
- [ ] Engage every new follower/holder personally. A 20-person real community beats 2,000 bots.
- [ ] Host one simple, honest event: run a **provably-fair giveaway of a small, self-funded prize**
      (or a partner-sponsored one) USING our own tool — it demonstrates the product AND grows the
      community. (Non-custodial; a modest, real prize you can honor — never a fake one.)

**BD → first integration (FOUNDER)**
- [ ] Follow up once with Week-1 contacts. Aim for ONE concrete integration: a project using a draw,
      a dev trying the scan API, or a community embedding the badge.
- [ ] For anyone hitting the API repeatedly, introduce the holder-gated key (real token demand).

**End of Week 2 — realistic targets (not guarantees):**
- A handful (5–20+) of real holders, not 2.
- Non-zero volume on some days (from real buyers, never faked).
- 1+ outside integration or repeated API user.
- A live giveaway that showcased the product + grew the TG.
- A short list of warm BD conversations for revenue.

## What NOT to do (these are how micro-caps die)

- ❌ Wash trading / bots to fake volume or holders.
- ❌ Paying for fake hype, fake KOL shills, or "guaranteed" pump groups.
- ❌ Promising returns, price targets, or "next 100x."
- ❌ Announcing a buyback/audit before it's real (both are prepared but gated on revenue/commission).

## After 2 weeks — the real flywheel

Usage → community → holders → first revenue → **buyback→burn (runbook ready) + audit (kit ready)** →
credible value accrual → more holders. It's a grind, and it's the only honest path. The product and
the trust are already built; from here it's distribution, people, and revenue — the founder-led work
no amount of code replaces.
